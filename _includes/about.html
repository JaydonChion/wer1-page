Who are we? Why choose us?

We are a group of startup veterans, accountants, product managers and engineers with more than 10 years of experience.
We understand it is an uphill battle when starting a company (probably steeper than our logo 😄). The sheer challenge a growing startup takes on can get overwhelming. Trust us, we have been there ourselves and we felt the pain. We were always wondering why there was no one-stop service provider helping the startup community to take care of the nitty-gritty daily administration?
Hence, WER1 is established to give the necessary support a startup needs from initial to growth phase. We give advice on tax, grants (that most startups overlook) or even sharing new venture and management experiences, well, for FREE.
